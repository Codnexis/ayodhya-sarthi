Popular Destinations

Ayodhya is one of the most sacred cities in India and is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Ram. The city attracts millions of pilgrims and tourists every year.

Ram Janmabhoomi Mandir

The grand Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Temple is the spiritual center of Ayodhya and one of the most visited pilgrimage destinations in India.

Read More

Hanuman Garhi

A famous temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman. Visitors climb several steps to receive blessings and enjoy beautiful views of Ayodhya.

Read More

Kanak Bhawan

Known for its magnificent architecture, Kanak Bhawan is associated with Lord Ram and Goddess Sita and remains a major attraction.

Travel Guide

Helpful information for first-time visitors.

Best Time To Visit

October to March is considered the best period to visit Ayodhya. The weather remains pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.

Local Transportation

Auto-rickshaws, e-rickshaws, taxis and buses are easily available throughout the city.

Travel Tips

Carry comfortable footwear, keep drinking water and follow temple guidelines during visits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions asked by tourists visiting Ayodhya.

What is the best time to visit Ayodhya?
October to March is generally considered the best time because the weather remains pleasant and suitable for sightseeing and pilgrimage activities.
How can I reach Ram Janmabhoomi?
You can reach Ram Janmabhoomi through local auto-rickshaws, e-rickshaws, taxis and public transportation available throughout Ayodhya.
Are hotels available near major temples?
Yes, numerous hotels, guest houses and dharamshalas are available near Ram Janmabhoomi, Hanuman Garhi and other major attractions.
Can I calculate transport fares before travelling?
Yes. Use the Fare Calculator section to estimate taxi, auto-rickshaw, e-rickshaw and bus fares.
Is emergency assistance available?
Yes. Ayodhya Sarthi provides emergency contacts including police, ambulance, fire brigade and tourist helpline information.
