Kanak bhawan

Kanak Bhawan (the "House of Gold") in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, is a highly revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Rama and Goddess Sita

History

Kanak Bhawan, located in Ayodhya, is a revered temple dedicated to Lord Rama and Goddess Sita. According to Hindu mythology, it was originally a golden palace gifted to Sita by Queen Kaikeyi immediately after her wedding.
