How Complaint Resolution Works

1. Complaint Registration

Submit complete complaint details through the online complaint portal.

2. Verification Process

Our support team reviews the complaint and verifies the submitted information.

3. Investigation

Authorities investigate the reported issue and gather necessary evidence.

4. Resolution

Appropriate action is taken and the complainant is informed.
